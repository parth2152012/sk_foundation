Enlightenment Program

Spreading awareness and knowledge to foster an informed, healthy, and empowered society.

Program Overview

The Enlightenment program is dedicated to spreading knowledge and awareness on critical issues that affect community well-being. We believe that an informed community is an empowered one. Through a series of workshops, campaigns, and interactive sessions, we tackle subjects ranging from health and hygiene to financial literacy and digital skills. Our goal is to equip individuals with the practical knowledge they need to make better decisions, improve their quality of life, and contribute positively to their communities.

Key Activities

Health & Sanitation

Conducting workshops on preventive healthcare, maternal and child health, nutrition, and the importance of clean water and sanitation.

Financial Literacy

Organizing drives to promote banking services, savings habits, and responsible financial management among rural households.

Digital Literacy

Providing basic computer and internet training to bridge the digital divide, opening up new avenues for information and opportunity.

Impact Story: A Healthier Tomorrow

"The health workshops conducted by Saksham Foundaion were an eye-opener. We learned so much about hygiene and nutrition that we never knew before. Simple practices like handwashing have drastically reduced illnesses in our families. The sessions on financial planning also helped me start a small savings group with other women in my village. We feel more in control of our lives now."
