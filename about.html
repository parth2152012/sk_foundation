About Us

Empowering communities through sustainable solutions and education since 2010.

Our Story

Saksham Foundation was established in 2010 with a vision to create sustainable change in underserved communities across India. What began as a small initiative to provide solar lighting in rural villages has grown into a comprehensive organization addressing education, healthcare, sustainable energy, and women's empowerment.

Our journey has been guided by the belief that lasting change comes from empowering communities with the tools, knowledge, and resources they need to become self-sufficient. We work closely with local leaders and community members to develop solutions that address their specific needs and challenges.

Today, Saksham Foundation operates in over 100 villages across 5 states in India, impacting the lives of more than 50,000 people. Our dedicated team of professionals and volunteers continues to expand our reach and deepen our impact, guided by our core values of integrity, innovation, and inclusivity.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower underserved communities through sustainable solutions, quality education, and accessible healthcare, enabling them to build a better future for themselves and future generations.

Our Vision

A world where every community has equal access to resources, opportunities, and the tools needed to thrive sustainably, with dignity and self-reliance.

Our Core Values

These principles guide our work and define our approach to creating sustainable change.

Integrity

We maintain the highest ethical standards in all our actions, ensuring transparency, accountability, and honesty in our operations and relationships.

Innovation

We embrace creative thinking and innovative approaches to solve complex challenges, constantly seeking better ways to serve communities and create lasting impact.

Inclusivity

We value diversity and ensure that our programs are accessible to all, regardless of gender, religion, caste, or socioeconomic status, promoting equality and respect.

Collaboration

We believe in the power of partnerships and work closely with communities, governments, and other organizations to maximize our impact and create sustainable solutions.

Sustainability

We design programs that create lasting change, focusing on solutions that communities can maintain and build upon long after our direct involvement ends.

Empowerment

We focus on building capacity and transferring knowledge, enabling individuals and communities to take ownership of their development and become agents of change.

Our Journey

A decade of growth, learning, and impact in communities across India.

2010

Saksham Foundation was established with a focus on providing solar lighting solutions to rural villages in Rajasthan.

2012

Expanded our work to include educational programs, establishing our first learning center in a remote village.

2014

Launched our women's empowerment initiative, providing vocational training and microfinance support to women entrepreneurs.

2016

Expanded operations to three additional states and established partnerships with government agencies and international organizations.

2018

Introduced our healthcare initiative, providing mobile medical services to remote communities with limited access to healthcare.

2020

Celebrated our 10th anniversary and launched our digital literacy program to bridge the digital divide in rural communities.

2022

Reached the milestone of impacting over 50,000 lives across 100+ villages and launched our sustainable agriculture initiative.
