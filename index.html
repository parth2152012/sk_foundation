Our Mission

At Saksham Foundaion, our mission is to empower individuals and communities through education, innovation, and sustainable development. We are committed to fostering a future where everyone has the opportunity to thrive and contribute positively to society.

Our Journey

2015

Saksham Foundaion was established with a mission to empower communities through education.

2017

Launched our first solar energy project, bringing sustainable power to rural communities.

2019

Expanded our education programs to reach over 1,000 students across multiple regions.

2021

Established community support centers providing resources and training for sustainable development.

2023

Celebrating our impact with over 5,000 students educated and 25 communities served.
